The First Honda Car: Honda T360

Honda entered the automobile industry with the T360, a small, innovative utility truck introduced in 1963. This was Honda's very first production vehicle and marked the beginning of Honda's journey in the automotive world. The T360 was renowned for its compact design, reliable performance, and unique character, paving the way for future generations of Honda vehicles.
